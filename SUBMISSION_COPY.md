## Project Name

PitchTruth AI

## One-Sentence Summary

PitchTruth AI helps fans and analysts understand how World Cup pitch conditions affect ball speed, player safety, and tactical fairness using explainable AI.

## Problem Statement

World Cup 2026 matches will be played in several stadium environments that may rely on temporary or hybrid grass systems. When pitch conditions affect ball roll, traction, fatigue, or injury risk, fans and even analysts often struggle to understand what is happening. PitchTruth AI addresses that gap by translating pitch science into clear, accessible soccer insight.

## Solution Description

PitchTruth AI is a web experience with a separate landing page and dashboard. Users can choose a stadium profile, ask focused match-surface questions, and receive both a fan-friendly explanation and a more technical analyst explanation. The system is designed to make surface behavior, fairness concerns, and player complaints more understandable before and during a match.

## AI And Technical Approach

The project uses AI as the core product experience. Stadium profiles and pitch-science knowledge are combined with a structured reasoning flow. Docling is used to prepare source material, LangFlow documents the orchestration pipeline, and Granite is used for the fan-facing explanation layer through OpenRouter. The application is built with Next.js and exposes a working API route for analysis generation.

## Why This Matters

Pitch quality is an overlooked factor in soccer performance and fairness. By making this hidden layer understandable, PitchTruth AI helps fans, journalists, and coaches better interpret match speed, tactical changes, and player concerns in the context of the World Cup.

## Demo Highlights

- Responsive landing page introducing the World Cup pitch problem
- Stadium intelligence dashboard
- Five explainable soccer question flows
- Fan View and Analyst View modes
- Exportable report
