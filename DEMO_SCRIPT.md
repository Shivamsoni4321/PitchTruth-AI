# PitchTruth AI Demo Script

Target length: 2 minutes 15 seconds to 2 minutes 45 seconds

## Recording Checklist

1. Start the app and open `http://localhost:3000`.
2. Keep only one clean browser tab visible.
3. Use full-screen or a large browser window.
4. Turn on microphone audio if you are narrating live.
5. Test one full walkthrough before the final recording.

## Click-By-Click Demo Steps

1. Open the landing page.
2. Pause on the hero section for 5 to 7 seconds.
3. Click `Analyze Stadium` or `Get Started`.
4. In the dashboard, select `Lumen Field` or `MetLife Stadium`.
5. Pause briefly on the animated stadium preview.
6. Pause briefly on the animated pitch-science visualization.
7. Click `Why are players complaining about this venue?`
8. Show the `Fan View` answer.
9. Click `Analyst View`.
10. Point to the source labels under the answer.
11. Point to the weather chart and tactical fairness chart.
12. Scroll to the Research Library and IBM AI Pipeline sections.
13. Click `Export Report`.
14. End on the dashboard with a short closing statement.

## Opening

"PitchTruth AI helps people understand how soccer pitch conditions can change the match they are watching. For World Cup 2026, several stadiums will use temporary or hybrid grass systems, and that can affect ball speed, footing, fairness, and injury risk."

## Problem

"Today, when players complain about the field, fans hear the reaction but not the explanation. Our project translates stadium and surface conditions into plain-English insights."

## Show Landing Page

"This landing page introduces the problem, the stadium intelligence concept, and the IBM-supported AI workflow behind the product."

## Show Dashboard

"Inside the dashboard, we can choose a stadium profile, review the pitch-risk context, and ask focused questions about how the surface may affect the match."

"The dashboard is interactive. The venue profile, weather signals, tactical fairness view, and match explanation all update as we move through the experience."

## Ask A Question

Suggested path:

- Choose `Lumen Field` or `MetLife Stadium`
- Choose `Why are players complaining about this venue?`
- Show `Fan View`
- Switch to `Analyst View`

Narration:

"The system generates two layers of explanation: a fan-friendly answer and a more technical analyst answer. This keeps the product accessible while still grounded in pitch-science reasoning."

## Show Supporting Panels

"Around the explanation, the dashboard also visualizes stadium context, weather behavior, fairness tradeoffs, and pitch-science zones so the answer feels transparent rather than magical."

## Show IBM Tooling Evidence

"PitchTruth uses IBM-supported tooling in the workflow. Docling structures research inputs, LangFlow documents the orchestration pipeline, and Granite powers the fan-facing explanation layer through OpenRouter."

## Show Export

"Finally, the report can be exported, which makes the output easier to share with judges, analysts, journalists, or coaches."

## Close

"PitchTruth AI turns an invisible part of soccer into something understandable. It helps fans, analysts, and coaches better interpret fairness, performance, and player safety before and during the World Cup."
